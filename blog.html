Lionel Messi, The Greatest Player To Have Graced Football

Who is Lionel Messi?

Messi, born and raised in Argentina, displayed innate talent for playing football early on. Messi began his footballing career when he was only 8 years old with a local soccer club named Newell's Old Boys. He continued to play with Newell's old boys for 5 years, quickly ascending the ranks, and enventually getting noticed by top soccer clubs. He eventually got recruited in the year 2000, at the age of 13 years, by FC Barcelona; one of the worlds best soccer clubs at that time and still to this day. Messi thus continued his footballing career overseas in Spain.

Messi joined Barca's under 14s academy and began progressing through Barcelona's ranks at an astonishing rate, Climbing thought Barca C, Barca B, and the first team in record time. He made his first team debut in a friendly match at the age of 16! He appeared in his first official match when he was 17 years old and became a rugular feature of Barca's first team squad afterwards.

Physical Struggles

Born with what seems to be god given football skills, messi had a growth defect during his early career. Messi was shorter and weaker than the rest of the players around him, but what he lacked in physicality he made up in pure raw talent. Tought treatment was available, it would cost upwards of $900 per month for 2 years, a cost that he couldn't afford. It wasn't until Barcelona recruited him that he underwent growth hormone treatment. Barcelona took a gamble on him by investing so much money on Messi, but in retrospect it was definatly worth it.

Shooting Accuracy

Messi's average shot attempt has a .182 chance of being goal, making him the 9th most efficient shooter in the sport. However, when taking shot volume into consideration, he is the top most efficient shooter of anyone with a similar shot volume to goal ratio.

Passing

Messi is proficient at short and long passes. Coming from FC Barcelona's academy, it comes to no suprise that he is superb at one touch passing due to Barcelona's tiki-taka style of play. What many people over look is his long passes and vision. He is able to make a pass where one doesn't seem to exist or impossible to make. Some people even say that he has eye's behind his head or a sixth sense.

Speed

Thanks to his speed, Messi is able to shake off defenders with ease. He is able to accelarate to 19.3 mph in 2.3 seconds while dribbling. Messi makes use of this ability by decelerating and quickly accelarating during a dribble to leave oponents behind.

Free Kicks

Messi made himself into the world's most dangerous free kick takers. He was not always good at free kicks, but over the past couple of years he has proved himself to be among the top. He scores about 8% of the time, compared to all player's average of 5%.

Fair Player

Messi doesn't dive like most players and continues to play even when fouled. You rarely see Messi diving. Honesty is Messi's style of play.

Dribbling

What seperates Messi from the rest is his quick change of direction. His ability to twist and turn his body, come to a quick stop during max sprints, and accelerate to top speed in a short time makes messi a nightmare to defend against. To make things worse, he is one of the few palyers in history able to keep the ball "glued" to his feet, even at a full sprint. His abilities allow him to take on multiple defenders with ease and high efficiency rates.
